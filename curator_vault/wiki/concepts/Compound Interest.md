# Compound Interest

The addition of interest to the principal sum of a loan or deposit, or in other words, interest on interest.

## Key Perspectives

### Morgan Housel (The Psychology of Money)
- Compound interest is the most powerful force in finance
- "Time is the most powerful force in investing"
- Good investing isn't necessarily about earning the highest returns
- Good investing is about earning pretty good returns that you can stick with for a long period of time

### Warren Buffett (mentioned in multiple books)
- "My wealth has come from a combination of living in America, some lucky genes, and compound interest"
- Started investing at age 11, but most wealth came after age 50
- Patience is essential for compounding to work

## The Mathematics

- 10% annual return doubles money every ~7 years
- Small differences in return rates create massive differences over decades
- Starting early matters more than contribution amount

## Applications

- **Investing**: Reinvesting dividends and gains
- **Skills**: Small daily improvements compound over time
- **Knowledge**: Learning builds on previous knowledge
- **Relationships**: Trust compounds through consistent behavior
