# Deep Work

Professional activities performed in a state of distraction-free concentration that push your cognitive capabilities to their limit.

## Key Perspectives

### Cal Newport (Deep Work)
- Deep work is becoming increasingly rare and valuable
- The ability to perform deep work is the superpower of the 21st century
- Shallow work (email, meetings) dominates but produces little value
- Deep work creates new value and improves skills

### Cal Newport (So Good They Can't Ignore You)
- Career capital comes from rare and valuable skills
- Deep work builds career capital
- Skills trump passion in career success
- Craftsman mindset > Passion mindset

## Core Principles

1. **Work deeply** - Schedule focused blocks of time
2. **Embrace boredom** - Don't seek constant stimulation
3. **Quit social media** - Remove shallow distractions
4. **Drain the shallows** - Minimize low-value activities
5. **Ritualize** - Create routines that support deep work

## Benefits

- Produce higher quality work in less time
- Learn complex skills faster
- Stand out in a distracted world
- Achieve flow states more frequently
