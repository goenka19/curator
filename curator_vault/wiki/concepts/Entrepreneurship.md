# Entrepreneurship

The process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business.

## Key Perspectives

### Phil Knight (Shoe-Dog)
- "The cowards never started, and the weak died along the way"
- Beginner's mind has many possibilities
- Crazy ideas can become reality with persistence
- Building Nike from $50 loan to global empire

### Peter Thiel (Zero to One)
- Monopoly beats competition
- Secrets exist and can be discovered
- Start with a small market and expand
- Technology enables 0 to 1 innovation (not 1 to n)

### Robert Kiyosaki (Rich Dad Poor Dad)
- Build businesses and assets, not just a career
- The rich build systems; the poor trade time for money
- Entrepreneurship is the path to financial independence

## Core Principles

1. **Start before you're ready** - Perfect is the enemy of good
2. **Solve real problems** - Value creation comes from problem-solving
3. **Iterate quickly** - Fail fast and learn
4. **Build a moat** - Sustainable competitive advantage
5. **Focus on distribution** - A great product without distribution fails
