# Wealth Building

The process of accumulating assets and resources over time to achieve financial security and independence.

## Key Perspectives

### Robert Kiyosaki (Rich Dad Poor Dad)
- Build assets that generate passive income
- The rich don't work for money; money works for them
- Financial education > Academic education
- Assets vs. Liabilities distinction

### Morgan Housel (The Psychology of Money)
- Wealth is what you don't see (suppressed consumption)
- Getting wealthy and staying wealthy are different skills
- Financial independence = passive income > expenses
- Compounding is the key to wealth

### Phil Knight (Shoe-Dog)
- Wealth comes from building something valuable
- Entrepreneurship as wealth creation vehicle
- Crazy ideas can become billion-dollar companies

## Core Principles

1. **Pay yourself first** - Save before spending
2. **Build assets** - Acquire income-generating assets
3. **Use leverage wisely** - Debt for assets, not consumption
4. **Long-term thinking** - Wealth compounds over decades
5. **Financial education** - Understand money, accounting, investing
