> "purchases made by using his formula would be bargains—bargains created by Mr. Market practically giving away businesses at unreasonably low prices. Graham suggested that by buying a group of these bargain stocks, investors could safely earn a high return without worrying about a few bad purchases and without doing complicated analysis of individual stocks."

## Insight

Buying a group of bargain stocks can help investors earn a high return without complex analysis, as a few bad purchases are offset by the overall portfolio performance.
