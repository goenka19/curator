> "The bad part about the disappearing dividend is that regular checks in the mail gave investors an income stream and also a reason to hold on to stocks during periods when stock prices failed to reward."

## Insight

The disappearance of dividend checks can lead to a lack of investor patience and perseverance during periods of underperforming stock prices.
