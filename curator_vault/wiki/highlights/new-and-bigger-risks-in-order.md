> "new and bigger risks in order to perpetuate return often repeats in a cycli- cal pattern. The motto of those who reach for return seems to be: “If you can’t get the return you need from safe investments, pursue it via risky investments.”"

## Insight

Investors often pursue risky investments to achieve desired returns when safe investments are insufficient, perpetuating a cyclical pattern of risk-taking.
