> "Just know that it’s fear that keeps most people working at a job: the fear of not paying their bills, the fear of being fired, the fear of not having enough money, and the fear of starting over. That’s the price of studying to learn a profession or trade, and then working for money. Most people become a slave to money"

## Insight

Fear of financial insecurity is a primary motivator for people to work, making them slaves to money.
