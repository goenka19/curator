> "The magic formula portfolio fared poorly relative to the market averages in 5 out of every 12 months tested.
For full-year periods, the magic formula failed to beat the market averages once every four years.* For one out of every six periods tested, the magic formula did poorly for more than two years in a row. During those wonderful 17
years for the magic formula, there were even some periods when the formula did worse than the overall market for three years in a row!"
