> "Does that sound like risky behavior? It would be if you had no idea how to read financial statements or evaluate individual businesses. But if you do have that ability, is buying a stake in your five favorite businesses enough? Would owning a stake in your eight favorites be better? I think most people, especially those who view stocks as long-term ownership stakes in actual businesses, would think that spreading that $1 million among investment stakes in five to eight bargain-priced businesses in varying industries would qualify as prudent behavior."
