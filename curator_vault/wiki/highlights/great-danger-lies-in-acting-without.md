> "Great danger lies in acting without having considered these questions. And, by the way, there’s no right choice between offense and defense. Lots of possible routes can bring you to success, and your decision should be a function of your personality and leanings, the extent of your belief in your ability, and the peculiarities of the markets you work in and the clients you work for."

## Insight

The choice between offense and defense in investing should be based on individual personality, belief in ability, and market peculiarities.
