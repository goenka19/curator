> "1. Stock  prices move around wildly over very short periods of time. This does not mean that the values of the underlying companies have changed very much during that same period. In effect, the stock market acts very much like a crazy guy named Mr.
Market.
2. It is a good idea to buy shares of a company at a big discount to your estimated value of those shares.
Buying shares at a large discount to value will provide you with a large margin of safety and lead to safe and consistently profitable investments."
