> "I'm too conceited. If you want to call it that. I don't make comparisons. I never think of myself in relation to anyone else. I just refuse to measure myself as part of anything. I'm an utter egotist.""

## Insight

The speaker's self-described egotism is rooted in a refusal to measure themselves against others, instead existing as a self-contained entity.
