# Cal Newport

Computer science professor and author focused on productivity, deep work, and career development.

## Books

- [[Deep Work]] (2016)
- [[So Good They Can't Ignore You]] (2012)

## Key Ideas

- Deep Work: Professional activities performed in distraction-free concentration
- Career capital: Rare and valuable skills that provide leverage
- Skills trump passion in career success
- Productivity requires focused attention, not just busyness
