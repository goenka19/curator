# Robert Kiyosaki

Author and educator known for financial literacy teachings, especially the concept of financial independence.

## Books

- [[Rich Dad Poor Dad]] (1997)

## Key Ideas

- Assets vs. Liabilities: Assets put money in your pocket
- The rich don't work for money; money works for them
- Financial education is more important than academic education
- Build businesses and investments, not just a career
- Pay yourself first
