# Peter Lynch

Legendary investor who managed the Fidelity Magellan Fund, achieving 29% annual returns from 1977 to 1990.

## Books

- [[One Up on Wall Street]] (1989)

## Key Ideas

- "Invest in what you know"
- Amateur investors have advantages over professionals
- Look for companies with simple business models
- Tenbagger stocks (10x returns) come from everyday observations
- Ignore market predictions, focus on company fundamentals
