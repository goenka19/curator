# Howard Marks

Co-founder of Oaktree Capital Management, known for his memos on investing and risk management.

## Books

- [[The Most Important Thing]] (2011)

## Key Ideas

- Second-level thinking: thinking differently and better than others
- Risk control is more important than return generation
- "You can't predict, but you can prepare"
- The pendulum swing of market psychology
- Most important thing is understanding risk
